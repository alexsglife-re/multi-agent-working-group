# Code Of Conduct

## Our Standard

Contributors should keep discussion practical, respectful, and focused on the
work. Assume good intent, but be precise when discussing safety, authority, and
workflow risks.

Examples of welcome behavior:

- Give specific, evidence-backed feedback.
- Explain workflow risk in plain language.
- Keep issues and pull requests scoped.
- Avoid sharing private or sensitive context.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks.
- Publishing another person's private information.
- Posting secrets, credentials, or private project material.
- Repeatedly derailing technical discussion.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and issues that violate this
code. Repeated or severe violations may lead to being blocked from the project.
